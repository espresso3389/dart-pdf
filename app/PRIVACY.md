# DartPDF privacy policy

_Last updated: 14 June 2026_

DartPDF is a PDF viewer and editor published by Railway Engineering Solutions
Pty Ltd ("we", "us"). This policy explains how the app handles your
information. The short version: **DartPDF does its work entirely on your
device and does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.**

## What we collect

**Nothing.** DartPDF has no analytics, no advertising, no accounts, and no
backend servers. We do not collect, log, or transmit any personal information,
usage data, or device identifiers.

## Your documents and OCR

PDFs you open, edit, annotate, sign, or save are processed **locally on your
device only**. Their contents are never uploaded to us or to any third party.
When you choose to share or export a document, it goes only to the destination
you pick (for example, another app via the system share sheet, or a file
location you select).

OCR also runs locally. Native builds may download the OCR model files on first
use, then recognition runs on your device. The web build downloads browser OCR
model/runtime files on first use and runs recognition in the browser process.
Your PDF pages and recognized text are not sent to a DartPDF OCR server.

## Device permissions

DartPDF requests access to files only so it can open the PDFs you choose and
save your changes back. This access is used solely to perform the action you
requested and for no other purpose.

## App preferences

Settings such as your theme, recent-file list, and tool styles are stored
locally on your device using the operating system's standard preference
storage. They never leave your device and are removed when you uninstall the
app or clear its data.

## Third parties

DartPDF does not embed third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
The app may open external links (such as its source-code repository) in your
browser when you explicitly tap them; those destinations have their own
privacy policies.

## Children

DartPDF is a general-purpose productivity tool and does not knowingly collect
any information from anyone, including children.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above and post
the revised policy at the same URL.
